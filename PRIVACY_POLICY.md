# Privacy Policy

This Chrome extension is designed to run entirely on your device.

## What this extension does

- Lets you map AWS account IDs to friendly account names.
- Displays those names in the AWS SSO portal UI.

## Data collection and external communication

- No data is collected by the developer.
- No analytics, telemetry, tracking, or logging is performed.
- No external API calls are made.
- No network requests are sent to developer-controlled servers.

## Where your data is stored

- Your account ID to name mappings are stored only in local Chrome extension storage (`chrome.storage.local`).
- This storage is managed by your browser on your current device.
- The extension does not export or transmit your mappings anywhere.

## Data sharing

- Your data is not sold.
- Your data is not shared with third parties.

## Permissions

- The extension uses the `storage` permission only to save and read your mappings.

## Changes

If this policy changes, updates will be published in this repository and extension listing.
